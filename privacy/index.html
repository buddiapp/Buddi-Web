Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Buddiapp Ltd ("Buddi", "we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our app, website, or any related services.

We've written this in plain English. If anything isn't clear, contact us at hello@buddiapp.co.

1. Who we are

Buddiapp Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales (company number: [TO BE INSERTED ONCE COMPANIES HOUSE CONFIRMS]). Our registered office is at [REGISTERED OFFICE ADDRESS]. We are registered with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) as a data controller.

If you have any questions about this policy, contact us at hello@buddiapp.co.

2. What information we collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you join the waitlist or create an account
  • Child information — your child's name, date of birth, and developmental details you choose to share within the app
  • Routine and check-in data — your responses to Buddi's daily check-ins, weekly questionnaires, and any events you choose to share (such as appointments, travel, or illness)
  • Usage data — how you interact with the app, including features used and screens visited
  • Device information — device type, operating system, and app version for technical support purposes
  • Subscription information — your subscription status and trial state, processed by Apple and managed via RevenueCat (we do not see or store your payment card details)
  • Communications — messages you send us via the contact form or email

3. How we use your information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and personalise the Buddi app experience
  • Generate personalised guidance and routines using AI (see Section 5 below)
  • Send you notifications and guidance relevant to your child's stage
  • Notify you when Buddi launches (waitlist only)
  • Improve our product through anonymised usage analytics
  • Respond to your enquiries and support requests
  • Process subscription payments and manage your account
  • Meet our legal and regulatory obligations

4. Legal basis for processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases under UK GDPR:

  • Contract — to provide the services you've signed up for, including subscription billing and personalised guidance
  • Legitimate interests — to improve our product, communicate with users about the service, and protect against fraud
  • Consent — for marketing communications and optional features, which you can withdraw at any time
  • Legal obligation — where required by law (such as retaining transaction records for tax purposes)

5. AI processing

Buddi uses artificial intelligence to provide personalised guidance, generate routines, and adapt to your child's specific patterns. To do this, we send relevant information about your child and your check-in responses to Anthropic, our AI provider, who processes this data on our behalf.

Important details about our AI processing:

  • Anthropic operates as our data processor under a strict data processing agreement
  • Anthropic does not use your data to train their AI models
  • Anthropic is based in the United States; we rely on Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the UK ICO to ensure your data receives equivalent UK GDPR protection
  • AI-generated guidance is reviewed by our system for safety and appropriateness, but you should always use your own judgement as a parent
  • You can opt out of AI personalisation by deleting your account, though this means Buddi cannot provide its core service

6. Children's data

Buddi is designed for and used by parents and guardians who are 18 or over. We do not collect personal data directly from children. We do collect limited information about children (such as name, date of birth, and developmental observations) which is provided to us by their parent or guardian for the purpose of personalising the parent's app experience.

Because this information relates to children, we treat it with particular care:

  • It is never shared with third parties for marketing purposes
  • It is not used to train AI models
  • It is retained only for as long as the parent's account is active
  • Parents can request deletion at any time
  • We do not create marketing profiles based on children's data

If you are a parent or guardian and wish to delete information we hold about your child, contact us at hello@buddiapp.co.

7. Sharing your information

We do not sell your personal data. We share your information only with trusted service providers who help us operate Buddi, all under strict data processing agreements compliant with UK GDPR.

Our current service providers include:

  • Apple (App Store, payment processing, push notifications) — global
  • Anthropic (AI processing for personalised guidance) — United States
  • Supabase (cloud hosting, database, authentication) — European Union
  • RevenueCat (subscription management) — United States
  • Google Workspace (business email, document storage) — United States with EU data residency where applicable
  • Resend (transactional email) — United States
  • Vercel (website hosting) — global infrastructure

We may also share your information with legal authorities where required by law or to protect the rights, safety, or property of our users.

8. International transfers

Some of our service providers, including Anthropic, RevenueCat, and Resend, are based in the United States. Where data is transferred outside the UK or European Economic Area, we rely on UK International Data Transfer Agreements and/or Standard Contractual Clauses approved by the UK Information Commissioner's Office to ensure your data receives an equivalent level of protection.

9. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as needed:

  • Account data — retained for the duration of your account, then deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Child information and routine data — deleted with your account; you may also delete individual children at any time
  • Subscription and payment records — retained for up to 7 years to meet UK tax and accounting obligations
  • Usage analytics — retained in anonymised form for up to 2 years
  • Support communications — retained for up to 3 years to provide consistent support
  • Waitlist email addresses — retained until you unsubscribe or for up to 2 years after launch, whichever comes first

10. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict our processing of your data
  • Request a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent
  • Object to automated decision-making and request human review
  • Lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO)

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@buddiapp.co. We will respond within 30 days. We will not charge a fee for reasonable requests, and we will not require disproportionate identification to verify your request.

11. Automated decision-making

Buddi uses AI to generate personalised routine suggestions. While these are automated, they do not produce legal or similarly significant effects on you, and they are designed to be reviewed and adjusted by you, the parent, at any time. You always have the final say on whether to follow Buddi's suggestions.

If you'd prefer to discuss Buddi's suggestions with a human, you can contact us at hello@buddiapp.co.

12. Privacy by design

Buddi is built with privacy as a core principle. We collect only the minimum data needed to provide our service, we store data in encrypted form, we never use child or parent data for advertising, and we never sell your data.

13. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies necessary for the site to function correctly (such as remembering your preferences and maintaining your session). We do not use advertising, marketing, or tracking cookies.

If we add analytics in future, we will update this policy and add a cookie consent banner before any non-essential cookies are set. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time.

14. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) for all data in transit
  • Encrypted storage of data at rest
  • Access controls limiting who can view your data within our team
  • Regular security reviews of our infrastructure
  • Data processing agreements with all third-party providers

No system is completely secure. If we ever experience a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify you and the ICO within 72 hours as required by UK GDPR.

15. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by email or through the app. The "last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision. Continued use of Buddi after a policy update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

If you have any questions or concerns about how we handle your data, contact us at hello@buddiapp.co.
